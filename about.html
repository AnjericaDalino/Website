About Us

University of Baguio (UB) is a private university located in the City of Baguio in the Cordillera Administrative Region. It was established in 1948 by Dr. Fernando Gonzaga Bautista as the Baguio Technical Institute. Since its foundation, the institution has marked its name as one of the top universities of the region.

University of Baguio High School (UBHS) is a private educational institution in Baguio City offering junior and senior high school education. It is affiliated with the University of Baguio (UB) and recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd). At present, it offers a regular high school program as well as a Special Program for the Arts and Sciences (SPAS). For its senior high school curriculum, University of Baguio High School currently provides Academic, Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL), Sports, and Arts and Design.

The University of Baguio High School, popularly known as UB High School, is the mother-department of the University of Baguio. The high school department was established at the same year as university: 1948. The preparatory school holds and joins many extracurricular activities like The Yuletide bliss, and High school day.
Many athletes attend UB Prep. and compete in many local, regional, national, and international competitions. University of Baguio Preparatory High School has a girls volleyball team that is known to participate in many tournaments in Baguio.
